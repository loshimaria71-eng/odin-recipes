Tavë Kosi

Home

Description

Tavë Kosi is a traditional Albanian dish made of lamb or rice baked in a creamy, tangy yogurt sauce. It's one of Albania's most beloved comfort foods, often served on special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lamb (or rice as alternative)
  • Yogurt
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Boil the lamb until tender.
  3. Mix yogurt, eggs, and flour into a smooth sauce.
  4. Place the lamb in a baking dish and pour the sauce over it.
  5. Bake for 40-50 minutes until golden on top.
