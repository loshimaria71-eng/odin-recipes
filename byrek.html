Byrek

Home

Description

Byrek is a traditional Albanian pastry made with thin layers of dough filled with cheese, meat, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Phyllo dough
  • Feta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the feta cheese with eggs.
  3. Layer the phyllo dough with the cheese mixture.
  4. Brush with melted butter.
  5. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden.
