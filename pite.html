Pite me Spinaq

Home

Description

Pite me Spinaq is a traditional Albanian pie made of thin layers of dough filled with fresh spinach, eggs, and cheese. It's a comforting dish often enjoyed for breakfast or as a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Phyllo dough
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Wash and chop the spinach.
  3. Mix spinach, feta cheese, and eggs in a bowl.
  4. Layer the phyllo dough with the spinach mixture.
  5. Brush the top with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden.
